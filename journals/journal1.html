I coded this instead of texting back

March 25, 2025

Sometimes typing hurts less than talking

Lately I’ve been living in this weird space between wanting to be alone and craving connection. Like I’ll turn my phone off to breathe for a second, but then get sad no one’s hitting me up. I think I’m just tired—tired of showing up for people who don’t ask how I’m doing back. This isn’t a cry for help, more like… a sigh typed out. The kind you let out when no one’s around, but you still hope someone hears it.
